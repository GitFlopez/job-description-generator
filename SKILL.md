## What This Skill Does

Generates complete, bias-reduced, SEO-optimized job descriptions that attract qualified candidates and deter unqualified ones. Covers every stage of the hiring process from initial posting to structured interview prep.

Four prompts — one skill set that handles the full JD workflow:

1. **Full Job Description** — Complete posting with role summary, responsibilities, requirements, and culture fit
2. **Scorecard & Rubric** — Structured evaluation rubric aligned to the JD for consistent interviewing
3. **Outreach Message** — Personalized recruiter message for passive candidate outreach (LinkedIn/email)
4. **Offer Letter Template** — Professional offer letter with compensation, benefits, and start date language

---

## Inputs Required

For each prompt you'll provide:
- Job title
- Company name and industry
- Location (remote/hybrid/on-site + city/state)
- Team size and who role reports to
- 5-7 core responsibilities (bullet form is fine)
- Must-have requirements (years experience, credentials, skills)
- Nice-to-have requirements
- Salary range (optional but strongly recommended)
- 2-3 company culture facts
