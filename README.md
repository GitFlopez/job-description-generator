## Why This Beats Templates

Generic templates produce generic JDs. Generic JDs attract generic candidates — or flood your inbox with unqualified applications.

This skill uses structured prompts that enforce:
- Bias-reduction rules (no degree gatekeeping, no gendered language, no "rockstar")
- SEO best practices (title in H1, location keywords, searchable skills)
- Legal compliance notes (salary range laws, EEOC language, at-will statements)
- Outcome-based requirements ("managed 3+ engineers" vs. "3+ years experience")
