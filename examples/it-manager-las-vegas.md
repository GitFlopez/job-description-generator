## Output 1: Full Job Description

---

**IT Manager — SunStone Resorts**  
📍 Las Vegas, NV (On-site) | 💼 Full-Time | 💰 $95,000–$115,000/year

**About SunStone Resorts**  
SunStone Resorts operates three boutique casino-hotel properties on the Las Vegas Strip with 800 employees and 1,200 guest rooms. We're mid-market hospitality — not a mega-resort, not a motel. Our tech stack is where we compete: faster check-in, better loyalty data, and property systems that actually work during a Saturday night rush.

**About the Role**  
The IT Manager owns the day-to-day reliability of every system guests and employees touch — PMS, POS, network, and the 47 other things that break during check-in. In 90 days, you'll have mapped our infrastructure gaps, hired one Level 2 tech, and eliminated the top 3 recurring incidents from our helpdesk queue. This role reports to the CFO and has a seat at the table when we evaluate new property systems.

**What You'll Do**  
- Own uptime for PMS (Opera Cloud), POS (Micros Simphony), and 200-node network across 3 properties  
- Manage a team of 3 IT technicians — scheduling, mentoring, escalation point  
- Lead vendor relationships for telecom, hardware procurement, and managed security services  
- Run the helpdesk queue (avg. 60 tickets/week) — resolve Level 3 escalations within 4 business hours  
- Execute annual DR testing across all 3 properties; maintain RPO < 4 hours for core systems  
- Manage $1.2M IT budget — track actuals monthly, present variance to CFO quarterly  
- Evaluate and implement 2 technology upgrades per year (currently scoping mobile key and self-check-in kiosk)  

**What You Bring**  

*Required:*  
- Managed an IT team of 2+ people in a 24/7 operational environment (hospitality, retail, healthcare, or similar)  
- Hands-on experience with enterprise networking (Cisco/Meraki preferred) — can troubleshoot Layer 2/3 issues without escalating  
- Administered a property management system or similarly complex multi-tenant platform  
- Managed vendor contracts and hardware refresh cycles with a defined budget  
- Available for on-call escalation rotation (1 week in 4)  

*Preferred:*  
- Hospitality experience — PMS/POS familiarity cuts onboarding time significantly  
- CompTIA Network+ or equivalent certification  
- Experience with physical security systems (access control, CCTV)  
- Bilingual English/Spanish  

**Why Join Us**  
- $95,000–$115,000 base DOE + annual performance bonus (target 8%)  
- Medical, dental, vision — SunStone pays 80% of employee premium  
- 401(k) with 4% company match, vesting after 1 year  
- 15 days PTO + 8 company holidays + 1 floating day in year 1 (accrues to 20 days after year 3)  
- Free parking at property + 50% F&B discount at all three resorts  
- Direct access to CFO — this is not a buried IT department  

**Our Commitment to Inclusion**  
SunStone Resorts is an Equal Opportunity Employer. We hire based on what you can do, not where you went to school or how long your resume is. If you meet the core requirements and are strong in the areas that matter most, we want to hear from you. We are actively working to build a team that reflects the diversity of the Las Vegas community we operate in.

**How to Apply**  
Submit your resume and a 3-sentence note on the most complex system outage you've resolved. We review applications on a rolling basis and respond within 5 business days.
